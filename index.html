About

What Is AgentPay?

AgentPay is a Solana-native token designed around one idea: your wallet should work for you. Every trade generates real $USDC rewards, streamed on-chain to every holder automatically — no staking, no claiming, no waiting.

Built on Swarms, powered by Solana, and backed by a community of holders who believe passive income should be simple, transparent, and unstoppable.

How It Works

Simple as 1, 2, 3, 4

01

Buy $AGENTPAY

Purchase $AGENTPAY on any Solana DEX. Any amount qualifies for rewards proportional to your holdings.

02

Hold in your wallet

No staking or locking required. Just keep $AGENTPAY in your Solana wallet and you're eligible.

03

Trading generates fees

Every buy and sell of $AGENTPAY generates a fixed fee percentage that goes into the reward pool.

04

USDC hits your wallet

Every 5 minutes, the pool is converted to $USDC and distributed on-chain to all holders automatically.
